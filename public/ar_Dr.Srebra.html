Details

Dr. Srebra Golubovik is a highly experienced Registrar in Physical Medicine & Rehabilitation, with over 25 years of clinical expertise in managing and treating musculoskeletal and neurological disorders. Throughout her distinguished career, Dr. Golubovik has helped countless patients enhance mobility, alleviate pain, and restore functional independence through a diverse range of advanced rehabilitative therapies and techniques. Her holistic approach to rehabilitation focuses on not only physical recovery but also overall well-being, ensuring that every patient receives personalized, comprehensive care tailored to their unique needs.
