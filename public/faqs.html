What services do you offer?
• We provide a range of services, including primary care, specialized consultations, diagnostic imaging, laboratory services, physiotherapy, nutrition and fitness programs.
How do I schedule an appointment?
Appointments can be scheduled online through our website, via phone, or in person at our reception desk
Do you accept insurance?
• Yes, we accept a variety of insurance plans. Please contact our billing department for more details on coverage.
What should I bring to my appointment?
• Please bring your Civil ID, insurance card, and any relevant medical records or medications.
Is there parking available at your facility?
Yes, we have ample parking available for our patients and visitors.

Our staff is fluent in multiple languages to accommodate our diverse patient community. Please inquire if you have specific language needs.
How do you ensure patient confidentiality?
• We adhere to strict privacy policies and comply with all regulations to protect your personal health information.
