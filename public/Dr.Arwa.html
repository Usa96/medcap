Details

Dr. Arwa is a highly respected cardiologist with a distinguished career spanning over 40 years. Born and raised in the Middle East, she pursued her passion for medicine at prestigious institutions worldwide. After obtaining her Ph.D. in Cardiology and Internal Medicine, she continued to specialize in metabolic and nutritional medicine, further enhancing her expertise. Fluent in Arabic, English, and Russian, Dr. Arwa's multilingual abilities allow her to connect deeply with a diverse range of patients. Throughout her career, she has earned numerous accolades for her commitment to patient care and medical excellence. Her approach combines cutting-edge medical technology with a personalized, compassionate touch, ensuring her patients receive the highest standard of care. With a wealth of knowledge and experience, Dr. Arwa is board-certified by the American Board and continues to lead advancements in cardiovascular health, prevention, and treatment strategies. She remains dedicated to empowering her patients through education and holistic care, making a profound impact on the lives of many.
