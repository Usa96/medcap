  3. Dr. Muthanna Sartawi

Details

With over 20 years of experience in orthopaedic surgery, Dr. Muthana Sartawi is a renowned Consultant Orthopaedic Surgeon specializing in adult reconstruction and general orthopaedics. He is widely recognized as one of the pioneers of one-day joint replacement surgery in both the Central Illinois area and the Middle East. Dr. Sartawi has performed nearly 3,000 total knee replacement surgeries using his innovative technique, achieving a remarkable success rate of 98% in both the U.S. and the GCC region.
